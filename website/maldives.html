1. Veligandu Island Beach

Famous for its crystal-clear waters, powdery sand, and luxurious overwater bungalows, Veligandu Island Beach is a dream destination for relaxation and natural beauty.

2. Male

The bustling capital city of Male offers a unique mix of traditional Maldivian culture and urban life. Highlights include the Islamic Centre, fish market, and local bazaars.

3. Hulhumale Island

This artificial island is a blend of modern development and serene beaches. A must-visit for snorkeling enthusiasts and those looking for peaceful walks by the ocean.

4. Maafushi

A perfect escape for budget travelers, Maafushi is known for its friendly locals, cozy guesthouses, and vibrant marine activities like snorkeling and fishing.

5. Utheemu

Located in the northern Haa Alif Atoll, Utheemu is rich in history, being the birthplace of the Maldivian national hero Sultan Mohamed Thakurufaanu.

5-6 Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Male, check into your resort, and enjoy a sunset dinner on the beach.

Day 2: Explore Male’s landmarks, including the Grand Friday Mosque and the local fish market.

Day 3: Take a day trip to Veligandu Island for snorkeling and relaxation.

Day 4: Visit Maafushi for local experiences, watersports, and beach fun.

Day 5: Explore Hulhumale and its pristine beaches. Enjoy a romantic evening on a private sandbank.

Day 6: Relax at your resort or take a short excursion to Utheemu before departure.
