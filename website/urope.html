1. Eiffel Tower - Paris, France

One of the most iconic landmarks in the world, the Eiffel Tower offers panoramic views of Paris from its observation decks.

2. Colosseum - Rome, Italy

The Colosseum, an ancient amphitheater in the heart of Rome, is a testament to the grandeur of the Roman Empire.

3. Amsterdam Canals - Netherlands

Known for its charming canals, Amsterdam offers picturesque boat rides, historic buildings, and a unique atmosphere.

4. Sagrada Familia - Barcelona, Spain

Designed by Antoni Gaudí, the Sagrada Familia is an extraordinary basilica known for its intricate architecture and ongoing construction.

5. The Swiss Alps - Switzerland

The Swiss Alps are a must-see for nature lovers, offering breathtaking views, hiking, skiing, and a tranquil atmosphere.

5-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris. Visit the Eiffel Tower and take a Seine river cruise.

Day 2: Explore the Louvre Museum and the Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Day 3: Travel to Rome. Visit the Colosseum and Roman Forum.

Day 4: Discover Amsterdam’s canals and visit the Anne Frank House.

Day 5: Travel to Barcelona and admire the Sagrada Familia and the Park Güell.
