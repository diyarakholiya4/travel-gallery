1. Paro

Paro (Dzongkha: སྤ་རོ་) is a town and the seat of Paro District in Bhutan's Paro Valley. It is a historic town with many sacred sites and historical buildings scattered throughout the area. Paro International Airport, Bhutan's sole international airport, is also located here.

2. Thimphu

Bhutan’s capital city combines culture with a cosmopolitan vibe. Explore the Bhutan Postal Museum, tour the massive Thimphu Dzong, and observe traffic directed manually by policemen (there are no traffic lights in the entire country).

3. Gangtey Valley

Gangtey Valley is known for its 17th-century monastery, Gangtey Goemba, overlooking vast farmland. Enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, archery, birding, and more while taking in the valley's natural beauty.

4. Laya

Laya is Bhutan's highest village, accessible only by trekking. Experience the unique culture of the Indigenous Layap people, yak herders, and stunning views of the Himalayan peaks.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paro, visit the National Museum, and explore Paro Dzong.

Day 2: Drive to Thimphu, explore Buddha Dordenma, and enjoy Thimphu's city life.

Day 3: Travel to Gangtey Valley, visit Gangtey Goemba, and enjoy serene hikes.

Day 4: Trek to Laya, interact with locals, and take in breathtaking views.

Day 5: Return to Paro, relax, and enjoy Bhutanese cuisine.
