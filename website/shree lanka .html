1. Kandy

Kandy is a cultural and spiritual hub in Sri Lanka, home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and surrounded by lush green hills.

2. Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is an iconic ancient fortress and UNESCO World Heritage site with stunning views and remarkable frescoes.

3. Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is an ancient city rich with history and well-preserved ruins, including sacred temples and the revered Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi tree.

4. Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa, another ancient capital, is renowned for its historic sites, including royal palaces, Buddha statues, and picturesque ruins.

5. Yala National Park

Yala National Park is a wildlife haven famous for its leopards, elephants, and scenic landscapes along Sri Lanka’s southeastern coast.

5-6 Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo, transfer to Kandy, and visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic.

Day 2: Explore Kandy, then travel to Sigiriya to climb Lion Rock and explore the surrounding area.

Day 3: Visit Anuradhapura to discover its ancient ruins and sacred temples.

Day 4: Explore Polonnaruwa’s historic sites, including the royal palace and Buddha statues.

Day 5: Safari adventure at Yala National Park, then relax at nearby beaches.

Day 6: Return to Colombo, enjoy sightseeing and shopping before departure.
